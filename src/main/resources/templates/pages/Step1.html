Step 1

Drive your fist in and up. To try to get the foreign object dislodged, push your fist and hand into your diaphragm or stomach area. Use a quick j-shaped motion, in and then up. Repeat this movement multiple times, if necessary.

  • If this does not dislodge the foreign object very quickly, you need to try to add more force with a stable object.
